Overview
In this experiment we have gathered various participants to be able to study a certain bacteria that can synthesize protein, which consequently, can taste remarkably similar to beef. The micro-organisms examined for this study came from volunteers' navels. Through using the charts below, we can view participants demographic information, and well as data related to the bacteria within their navels.
